Welcome to the digital home of Nick and Krista Skytland. This is the place where we archive our thoughts, photos and other various effects.



Nick works at NASA and is currently researching how governments can apply the art and science of mass collaboration to help solve humanity's toughest challenges. Krista is a professional educator working to positively influence the next generation of Americans. We are blessed with two adventurous sons and an understanding dog. Collectively, we represent 9.32% of the total number of Skytlands currently living in the world today!

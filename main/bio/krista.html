Krista Skytland

"Let the wise hear and increase in learning, and the one who understands obtain guidance" - Proverbs 1:5

Krista Skytland (b. 10 May 1983) is a professional educator with a passion for developing the next generation of Americans. She is continually investing in those around her and has dedicated her professional life to helping others realize their full potential, whether it occurs in families, an organization, or a local community.

Skytland is currently a teacher at the Clear Creek Community Church in League City, TX, where she provides training, support and educational development to children in the local community. She also selflessly serves her family as a full time mom. Previously, Skytland served as a second grade teacher at Wedgewood Elementary in the Clear Creek Independent School District where she taught for six years.
